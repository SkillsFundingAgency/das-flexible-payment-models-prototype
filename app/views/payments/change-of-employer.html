If an apprentice you are training has moved to a new employer you should:

  1. Contact the previous employer to let them know that the record will be stopped.
  2. Speak to the new employer to agree new training dates and price.
  3. Ask the new employer to give you permissions to move the apprentice to their apprenticeship service account.

Once you have agreed these details you can continue to change the employer.
